Why Nostr

Social Networking is a crowded space. Each existing option has benefits and costs:

  • Centralized services (Twitter, Facebook, Truth Social)
    • Ease of use for ads and censorship
  • Decentralized or p2p social networks (Mastadon, SSB)
    • Free speech for complicated setup and expensive servers

Click here for more details on the shortcomings of existing solutions.

The nostr protocol enables creation of decentralized and reliable social networks.
